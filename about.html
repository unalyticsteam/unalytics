About Unalytics

The Unalytics research group at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Medellín campus, aims to provide and leverage study and research spaces to develop theoretical and practical capabilities in data analytics, machine learning, big data, artificial intelligence, and data engineering. We utilize programming languages oriented to data analysis such as Python and R to contribute to the academic and professional development of students.

Our focus includes modern data engineering practices, building robust data pipelines, cloud computing solutions, and implementing scalable data architectures that support advanced analytics and machine learning initiatives.
